Who We Are

Belize Marine & Mooring Services provides ship-assist/harbor tug services, including the docking/undocking of ships and ATBs. We are the exclusive provider at the Port Of Belize.

Our Mission

Professional Services

PRECISION SHIP ASSIST

Every day, BMMS tugs exercise precision to move tonnage and make critical maneuvers on ever-changing waters. Our clients are our top priority and ship-assist movement is what we do. We use only the best equipment and experienced crews to assist and dock vessels in the Port of Belize.

DREDGING

We provide precision capital and maintenance dredging to ensure your waterways remain deep, navigable, and safe. Our fleet features specialized equipment, including a Mud Cat 12-inch suction dredge and a 65-foot long-arm excavator paired with barges for maximum reach and accuracy. We also offer expert marine crane services and lead coastal restoration projects.

Offshore Vessel Support

We provide expert marine construction support and precision vessel positioning for complex offshore projects, including high-stakes subsea fiber optic installations. Our specialized tug and line boat services ensure stable station-keeping and seamless barge assistance.
