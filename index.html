Who We Are

Belize Marine & Mooring Services provides ship-assist/harbor tug services, including the docking/undocking of ships and ATBs, as well as Escort, Hold-In, and Stand-by services. We are the exclusive provider of tug services at the Port Of Belize.

Fleet

Captain Daniel

The Captain Daniel (formerly the J.A. Bisso II) is a veteran twin-screw tugboat that operates with a powerful repowered setup of two EMD 12-645 diesel engines. Delivering a combined 4,600 horsepower, this 125-foot vessel utilizes its high-torque displacement to provide a formidable bollard pull of approximately 50 tons. Originally built in 1965 and equipped with a double-drum towing winch, the tug remains a highly capable asset for both heavy ship-assist duties and offshore hawser towing.

George II

The GEORGE II is a high-performance twin-screw tugboat that bridges the gap between nimble harbor maneuvers and heavy-duty towing with its impressive 2,000 horsepower output. Dual Detroit Diesel 12V71 engines provide instant torque necessary for precision work. It serves as a versatile workhorse for complex barge-handling and ship-assist operations.

Services

Precision Ship Assist

Every day, BMMS tugs exercise precision to move tonnage and make critical maneuvers on ever-changing waters. Our clients are our top priority and ship-assist movement is what we do. We use only the best equipment and experienced crews to assist and dock vessels in the Port of Belize.

Dredging

We provide precision capital and maintenance dredging to ensure your waterways remain deep and safe. Our fleet features specialized equipment, including a Mud Cat 12-inch suction dredge and a 65-foot long-arm excavator. We also offer marine crane services and coastal restoration projects.

Offshore Vessel Support

We provide expert marine construction support and precision vessel positioning for complex offshore projects, including subsea fiber optic installations. Our specialized tug and line boat services ensure stable station-keeping and seamless barge assistance.
