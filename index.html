Who We Are

Belize Marine & Mooring Services provides ship-assist/harbor tug services, including the docking/undocking of ships and ATBs. We are the exclusive provider at the Port Of Belize.

Corporate Mission

"Our mission is to provide our customers with exceptional service, service that not only meets their needs but is always safe, timely, and efficient."

Fleet

CAPTAIN DANIEL

The Captain Daniel (formerly the J.A. Bisso II) is a veteran twin-screw tugboat that operates with a powerful repowered setup of two EMD 16-645 diesel engines. Delivering a combined 4,600 horsepower, this 125-foot vessel utilizes its high-torque displacement to provide a formidable bollard pull of approximately 50 tons.

George II

A high-performance twin-screw tugboat with 2,000 HP generated by dual Detroit Diesel 12V71 engines, providing precision maneuvering in tight harbor slips and versatile workhorse capabilities.

MR. G & MR. KHRIS

Essential 1,000 HP line boats specializing in barge shifting and securing tow lines with the immediate thrust and side-to-side agility required for massive barge fleets.

Services

PRECISION SHIP ASSIST

Every day, BMMS tugs exercise precision to move tonnage and make critical maneuvers on ever-changing waters. Our clients are our top priority and ship-assist movement is what we do. We use only the best equipment and experienced crews to assist and dock vessels in the Port of Belize.

DREDGING & RECLAMATION

We provide precision capital and maintenance dredging to ensure your waterways remain deep, navigable, and safe for even the largest commercial vessels. Our fleet features specialized equipment, including a Mud Cat 12-inch suction dredge for efficient sediment removal and a 65-foot long-arm excavator paired with barges and spotters for maximum reach and accuracy. Beyond excavation, we offer expert marine crane services and lead coastal restoration projects that repurpose materials to rebuild eroded shorelines and create sustainable land for development.

OFFSHORE VESSEL SUPPORT

We provide expert marine construction support and precision vessel positioning for complex offshore projects, including high-stakes subsea fiber optic installations. Our specialized tug and line boat services ensure stable station-keeping and seamless barge assistance, allowing for the accurate deployment of sensitive underwater infrastructure. With a focus on reliability and technical maneuverability, we serve as the vital link between surface operations and the seafloor to ensure every project is completed safely and on schedule.
