Who We Are

Belize Marine & Mooring Services provides ship-assist/harbor tug services, including the docking/undocking of ships and ATBs, as well as Escort, Hold-In, and Stand-by services. We are the exclusive provider of tug services at the Port Of Belize.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide our customers with exceptional service, service that not only meets their needs but is always safe, timely, and efficient.

FLEET

Captain Daniel

The Captain Daniel (formerly the J.A. Bisso II) is a veteran twin-screw tugboat that operates with a powerful repowered setup of two EMD 12-645 diesel engines. Delivering a combined 4,600 horsepower, this 125-foot vessel utilizes its high-torque displacement to provide a formidable bollard pull of approximately 50 tons. Originally built in 1965 and equipped with a double-drum towing winch, the tug remains a highly capable asset for both heavy ship-assist duties and offshore hawser towing.

George II

The GEORGE II is a high-performance twin-screw tugboat that bridges the gap between nimble harbor maneuvers and heavy-duty towing with its impressive 2,000 horsepower output. This substantial power is generated by dual Detroit Diesel 12V71 engines, a legendary configuration known for providing the instant torque and high-response throttle control necessary for precision work in tight slips. With its rugged build and enhanced power-to-weight ratio, the George II serves as a versatile workhorse, capable of tackling everything from complex barge-handling to demanding ship-assist operations with ease.

Line Boats

These compact line boats are the essential "connectors" of the waterway, specializing in the high-stakes tasks of shifting individual barges and securing tow lines. With a punchy 1000 horsepower configuration, they provide the immediate thrust and side-to-side agility required to move heavy steel into perfect alignment for larger tows. Their small footprint and high-torque engines make them the go-to assets for "getting lines"—handling the heavy rigging and wire work that keeps a massive fleet of barges acting as a single, stable unit.
